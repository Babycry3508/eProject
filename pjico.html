GENERAL INFORMATION ABOUT THE CORPORATION

- Corporation name: PG Insurance Corporation.
- Abbreviated name: PJICO.
- Charter capital: VND 800 billion. Currently, PJICO is one of the insurance enterprises with the largest charter capital in the Vietnam Insurance market.
- Headquarters: PJICO Tower - 01 Pham Van Bach Street – Cau Giay District – Hanoi – Viet Nam.
- Phone: (84-4) 3733 5588 - Fax: (84-4) 3733 6284.
- Website: www.baohiempvi.com.vn Email: baohiempvi@pvi.com.vn

20 years ago, on June 15, 1995, Insurance Joint Stock Company Petrolimex (now known as Petrolimex Insurance Joint Stock Corporation referred to as PJICO) officially established, is the first joint stock company operating in the field Insurance business in Vietnam market, founded by founding shareholders major economic organizations including the Vietnam National Petroleum Corporation (now Vietnam National Petroleum Group - Petrolimex) holds a controlling stake with 51% of the capital.

With a limited capital of only VND 33 billion, no facilities, The original staff was only 8 people, starting with the number "No" while The model of joint stock company is still very new and has not been implemented by economic organizations and people trust, insurance business is again a form of business based Customer trust foundation, so PJICO's operation met many difficulties.

By effort and determination with appropriate steps to penetrate enter the market, build trust with customer partners. From the original scale with 4 affiliated units in Hai Phong, Da Nang, Saigon, Can Tho, for 5 years First, PJICO has focused on developing mainly in important economic areas such as Quang Ninh (1996), Nghe An (1996), Quang Binh (1997), Hue (1996), Khanh Hoa (1997) ...; network organization to recruit personnel training staff members there, at the same time deploying the business, taking the revenue from the Petroleum unit as the basis of the original platform. This is also a medium PJICO phase has just made experience, focused on building operating institutions, laying the ground Foundation for future development strategy.

Striding fast

After the first 5 years of building an initial position in the market School, PJICO quickly plans a comprehensive development strategy on all field. Regarding financial potential, step by step increasing the charter capital: VND 70 billion (2002), 140 billion dong (2004), 300 billion dong (2006), 709 billion dong (2010) to improve the capacity business efficiency. About the organization, focus on developing the operational network together with synchronous and modern technical material foundations (headquarter, office transportation, working equipment ...) just create trust for customers just created favorable working environment for employees. New branches turn out life like Thai Nguyen, Ninh Binh, Ha Tinh, Quang Ngai and Vung Tau (2002); Phu-Tho, Bac Giang, Hai Duong, Thai Binh, Nam Dinh, Quang Nam, Binh Dinh, Phu Yen and Lam Dong, Dak Lak and the southwestern provinces (2003); Lao Cai, Hoa Binh and Vinh Phuc, Hung Yen, Gia Lai, Binh Thuan (2004) ... By 2010, PJICO had a network of 50 Unit operations basically cover the nationwide area with force the number of employees is 1,300 and has been firmly trained in insurance and business operations rich practical experience.

Regarding business, this is a breakthrough development period for PJICO market. With over 80 comprehensive insurance products in all operations Flexible business strategy, adapting to market, entrepreneurial style Dynamic, professional, PJICO quickly dominates the market with an increase average growth of 50 - 70% / year in 2002-2005 and below 30% / year in the years 2006-2010. By 2010, PJICO's revenue reaches 1,800 billion dong, 10 times more than in 2000, accounting for over 10% market share. Model Shares in insurance business were officially approved, gaining trust reliability of numerous customers, A series of key national projects PJICO have all participated in insurance such as: Petrolimex's ocean fleets, Vosco; National highway 1A project, Nghi Son, Hoang Mai and Tam Diep cement plants; The big hotel in Hanoi, Ho Chi Minh City such as Daewoo, Sheraton, Diamond Plaza; Dai Ninh, Se San and Cao Ngan thermal power plants; Can Tho and Bai Cau bridges Fire, Thanh Tri Bridge ...

Strong growth in revenue, accumulated financial resources, increase the value of assets to more than 3 times the charter capital, ensuring daily income the higher for employees (from VND 2 million / person / month in 2000 to more than 6 million VND / person / month 2010); ensure an average dividend of 9-12% / year for the shareholders east, becoming the business with the highest profit / capital ratio in the market, closed contributing over 100 billion VND to the state budget each year, actively participating in the work social welfare such as caring for VNAH mothers, building gratitude houses for the poor, support Agent Orange victims, support flood victims ...

Recognize PJICO's achievements that contribute to the economy and responsibility to the community and society, in 2010 the Party and State awarded the Lao Medal Class II and many certificates of merit and emulation flags of the Prime Minister and ministries concerned branches and People's Committees of provinces and cities across the country. for the Corporation.
