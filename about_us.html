Welcome to Safecarz Limited!

Safecarz Limited is a private, independently owned company based in Ho Chi Minh City, founded by its current Principal, Tuan Bui, in April 2001. Like most small businesses the company had humble beginnings at 214-77 Jamaica Avenue, Queens Village NY and sought to provide its customers with quality property, casualty, and life. In 2008 the company relocated to 593 CMT8, HCM City, and was able to provide insurance plans from top 4 biggest insurance companies in the world. Furthermore during this time the company decided to branch out into what is now its niche market, and began to write religious institutions on a small scale in 2009.
